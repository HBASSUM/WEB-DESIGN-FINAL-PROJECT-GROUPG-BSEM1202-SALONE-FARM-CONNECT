#  Salone Farm Connect

Digital Public Good (DPG) Project

Project Overview

Salone Farm Connect is a web-based agricultural platform developed to support farmers across Sierra Leone. The platform provides market price information, farming guidance, and opportunities for farmers to connect directly with buyers.

This project was developed as part of a Digital Public Goods (DPG) and Open Source Software initiative aimed at solving real-world problems in Sierra Leone.



Problem Definition

Problem Title

Limited Access to Agricultural Market Information and Buyers

Location

Sierra Leone

Who is Affected?

- Farmers
- Agricultural cooperatives
- Produce buyers
- Rural farming communities

Why is it Important?

Many farmers in Sierra Leone do not have access to current market prices and reliable buyers. As a result, they often sell products at unfair prices and lose income opportunities. Lack of information also affects productivity and decision-making.

# Proposed Solution

Salone Farm Connect provides a centralized platform where farmers can:

- Register their farming information
- View market prices
- Access farming advice
- Connect with potential buyers
- Obtain agricultural support information

The system improves communication between farmers and markets while promoting agricultural growth.
